Medical Parasitology Examination Portal

Comprehensive Assessment of Parasitology Knowledge

This portal provides access to the complete Medical Parasitology examination, divided into four specialized units. Each unit contains targeted questions to assess your understanding of specific parasite groups and their medical significance.

Student Information

Examination Instructions

  • Complete all four examination units to finish the comprehensive assessment.
  • Each unit has a time limit of 60 minutes (1 hour).
  • You must achieve a minimum score of 70% in each unit to pass.
  • Answer all questions in a unit before proceeding to the next one.
  • You will receive immediate feedback after selecting an answer.
  • Your progress will be saved automatically as you complete each unit.
  • Ensure you provide accurate information as it will be used for certification.
Important Notice: Please use your correct name, student ID, and email address as these will be recorded for examination records and future certification. Incorrect information may result in disqualification.
